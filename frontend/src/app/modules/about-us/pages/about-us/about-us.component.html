About Wanderer
The Wanderer Application is a travel companion platform where all enthusiastic travellers can find attractive and well-known places of attraction in their nearby locality with real-time map location. This application enriches the travel companions with more detailed information about the location through the reviews given by peer travellers who have previously visited that place. The core idea of this application is to multiply the travellers community where new travellers can get a fresh perspective of places through the memories shared by the other zealous travellers.
